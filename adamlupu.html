I help people optimize their learning.

I apply what we know about how human beings learn to the creation and validation of learning environments, tools, games, and professional training. I focus primarily on STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) education and its link to modern job skills and practices.

I've been a teacher, a filmmaker, a poet, a product manager, and a personal consultant. Throughout it all, I've been intensely curious about how people acquire knowledge, learn behaviors, adopt perspectives, and apply their evolving skills.

After discovering what I believe to be a profound and powerful method for communicating the nature of human learning, I'm now happy to call myself a Learning Architect. My mission: Empower people to deliberately and efficiently learn what they need in order to lead the lives they want to live.
